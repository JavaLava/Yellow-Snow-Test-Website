Just as every video is unique, every working relationship is different. For this reason, we won't give you a menu of products or proposals, and we won't talk turkey until we've had a natter with you about your business and your brand.

1. MEET THE TEAM
Yellow Snow prides itself on being people persons. We believe that face time is the most valuable commodity in any business relationship. We ensure that we fully understand the client's needs and offer exciting new concepts or innovations to build on that need.

2. DEFINE AND DESIGN
A Project Proposal is drawn up and projected tasks are discussed - any major changes to the formula will be identified here. Yellow Snow's expert concept team takes over to refine any rough aesthetics and by working closely with the client, settle on a final design.

3. CRAFT
The legwork begins. Yellow Snow will produce a timeline for the project and a series of staggered targets to meet. It is during this stage that Team Yellow Snow are least likely to be exposed to sunlight for great lengths of time.

4. FEEDBACK
Feedback is vital to the creation process, and will happen constantly as targets are set and hit. At the end of the project, a final review is carried out, addressing any amendments and completing the task.

5. CHRISTMAS COMES EARLY
Unless it's actually Christmas, of course. Yellow Snow delivers the project to your specifications, polished and ready to hit the ground running. The final piece cannot fail to make an impact.
